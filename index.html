This Month's Favorites

We asked and you told! Below are our customers top 4 favorite items on our menu!

Red Velvet Cupcake

Our famous red velvet cupcakes have a soft crumb, moist texture, hint of chocolate, and a gorgeous bright red color. Then they’re topped with tangy cream cheese.

Indiviual Apple Pie

Apple Crumb Pie is a delicious and easy pie that everyone absolutely raves over. A tender flaky crust filled with sweet juicy cinnamon kissed apples all topped of with a delicious crumb topping.

K's famous Chocolate Chip Cookies

This perfect chocolate chip cookie has a moist and fudge-like center that is just barely baked through. The caramelized exterior is crispy and crunchy, and substantial enough to contain the molten-lava-like center.

Very Berry Cheesecake

This cheesecake has a perfect buttery graham cracker crust. Topped with a smooth, creamy, and tangy cheesecake. Swirled with a sweet blueberry sauce! Top it with even more blueberry sauce and blueberries, for pure perfection!
